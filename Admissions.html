AdmissionAdmission Admission Admission

At Abiford University, we welcome students who are passionate about learning, growth, and contributing positively to society. Our admissions process is simple, transparent, and student-focused.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Proof of English proficiency (for international applicants)
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Valid identification (National ID or Passport)

Application Steps

  1. Fill out the admission form online.
  2. Upload all required documents.
  3. Pay the non-refundable application fee.
  4. Wait for confirmation and further instructions via email.
