Tuition & Fees

At Abigford University, we provide affordable world-class education. Below are the tuition rates for the academic year 2025/2026.

Tuition Breakdown

Program Local Students International Students Duration
Undergraduate Programs $3,000 / year $5,500 / year 4 years
Postgraduate Programs $4,500 / year $7,000 / year 2 years
PhD Programs $6,000 / year $9,500 / year 3–5 years
Short Courses / Certificates $500 – $1,000 $800 – $1,500 3–12 months

Payment Information

Payments can be made via bank transfer, online portal, or at the university bursary office. Flexible installment plans are available upon request.

Note: Additional fees may apply for accommodation, laboratory usage, and student services.
